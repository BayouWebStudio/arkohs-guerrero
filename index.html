About

Houston-based tattoo artist specializing in bold color work and Aztec/Mesoamerican-inspired designs. With over 20K followers and a passion for cultural artistry, I create large-scale sleeves and culturally rich pieces that tell stories through ink.


Known for vibrant color realism and intricate Aztec themes, each piece is custom-designed on iPad and brought to life with precision and artistry. Working out of Studio A in downtown Houston, I believe in the power of self-belief: "Cree en ti, duda del resto."
